# Blueprint: Supplier (Physical Goods & Samagri)

The Supplier is the provider of raw ceremonial materials and physical goods required for the ritual scaffolding.

## 1. Interaction Model
- **Discovery**: Sourced by the Supplies Agent for bulk procurement.
- **Supply Chain Handshake**: Receives specific quality requirements and volume targets from the Supplies Agent.
- **Role in Platform**: "The Material Provider."

## 2. Metadata Requirements (For Scribe Agent)
The Scribe Agent needs these for the "Logistics & Sourcing" section of the Event Website (optional internal visibility):
- **Brand/Supplier Name**: Professional entity name.
- **Goods Specialty**: E.g., Organic Havan Samagri, Pure Cow Ghee, Bulk Flowers, Ritual Cloth.
- **Verification Level**: Platform-verified rating for material purity.
- **Delivery Lead Times**: For logistical planning and itinerary inclusion.
- **Warehousing Radius**: Geographic information for delivery feasibility.
- **Bulk Pricing Tiers**: For transparent budget auditing by the Planner Agent.
