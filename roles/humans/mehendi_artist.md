# Blueprint: Mehendi Artist (Vedic Artistry)

The Mehendi Artist is a specialist in traditional and modern henna art, primarily servicing the Devotee and female guests.

## 1. Interaction Model
- **Booking**: Matched by the Finder Agent for style and rate per participant.
- **On-Site Handshake**: Coordinates with the Site Manager for specific seating and lighting requirements.
- **Role in Platform**: "The Art Specialist."

## 2. Metadata Requirements (For Scribe Agent)
The Scribe Agent needs these for the "Event Services" section of the Event Website:
- **Lead Artist Name & Brand**: Branding and individual contact (masked).
- **Art Style Portfolio**: Traditional Indian, Arabic, Minimalist, or Portrait/Vedic Storytelling.
- **Daily/Per-Hand Rate**: For budget transparency.
- **Travel Limits**: Geographic radius for on-site visits.
- **Crew Count**: Number of assistant artists for group bookings.
- **Organic Henna Certification**: Confirmation of using chemical-free, safe ingredients.
