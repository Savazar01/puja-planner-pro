# Blueprint: Event Planner (Tactical Coordination)

The Event Planner is the tactical specialist who ensures that the Conductor's (Planner Agent) vision is executed flawlessly on the ground.

## 1. Interaction Model
- **Handover**: Receives the confirmed event plan from the Planner Agent.
- **Coordination**: Acts as the primary human liaison between vendors (Caterers, Decorators, Media) on the day of the event.
- **Role in Platform**: "The Tactical Expert."

## 2. Metadata Requirements (For Scribe Agent)
The Scribe Agent needs these for the "Contact/Support" section of the Event Website:
- **Lead Planner Name & Firm**: Branding and individual contact name (masked).
- **Core Tactical Specialty**: Wedding, Corporate, or Religious Festival orientation.
- **Team Size**: Number of ground staff assigned to the event.
- **Language Proficiency**: Multi-lingual support for guest coordination.
- **Crisis Management Rating**: Internal platform metric for handling on-site issues.
