# Blueprint: Decorator (Theme & Ambience)

The Decorator is responsible for the visual and experiential transformation of the venue space.

## 1. Interaction Model
- **Creative Alignment**: Works with the Planner Agent and Devotee to translate the "Creative Vision" into a physical theme.
- **On-Site Coordination**: Works with the Location Manager to ensure the theme fits within the Agni-compliance and infra constraints.
- **Role in Platform**: "The Design Specialist."

## 2. Metadata Requirements (For Scribe Agent)
The Scribe Agent needs these to populate the "Theme & Decor" gallery on the Event Website:
- **Lead Designer Name & Firm**: Brand and individual contact (masked).
- **Theme Specialties**: E.g., Eco-Friendly Vedic, Traditional Royal, Minimalist Glassmorphic.
- **Inventory Highlights**: Specific props or Mandap designs available.
- **Setup/Breakdown Time**: Logistics for the itinerary.
- **Theme Portfolio**: Visual links to past work and renderings of proposed designs.
- **Sustainable Options**: Boolean for "Eco-friendly/Plastic-free" decoration availability.
