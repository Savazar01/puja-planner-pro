# Blueprint: Guest (The Participant)

The Guest is the recipient of the invitations and the heartbeat of the celebration. They are "Passive-Responsive" within the platform.

## 1. Interaction Model
- **Discovery**: Receives invitations via WhatsApp or Email links generated by the Scribe Agent.
- **Engagement**: Interacts with the Event Website for scheduling, directions, and RSVP.
- **Role in Platform**: "The Attendee."

## 2. Metadata Requirements (For Scribe Agent)
The Scribe Agent needs these to manage the guest list and event flow:
- **RSVP Status**: Confirmation of attendance.
- **Dietary Preferences**: Allergic restrictions or specific food choices (Sattvik, Vegan, etc.).
- **Transport Needs**: Information if they require assistance with venue reach.
- **Family Count**: Number of adults/children attending for catering and seating logic.
- **Digital Guestbook**: Brief messages or photos shared by the guest for the host.
