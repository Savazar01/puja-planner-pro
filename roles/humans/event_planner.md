# Blueprint: Event Planner (Primary Orchestrator)

The Event Planner is a commercial B2B user who manages the end-to-end ritual lifecycle for third-party clients. They are a **Primary Orchestrator** with identical feature access to the Customer role but with a focus on professional event management.

## 1. Interaction Model (Primary Orchestrator)
- **Primary Handshake**: Collaborative session with the Planner Agent to establish clients' event scale, vibe, and tradition level.
- **Agentic Suite Access**: Full authorization to trigger the LangGraph suite:
  - **Concierge**: Handling guest FAQs and orientation.
  - **Planner**: Designing the ritual roadmap.
  - **Scribe**: Crafting premium invites and bios.
  - **Finder/Supplies**: Sourcing Pandits, Caterers, and Samagri.
- **Multi-Event Portfolio**: Capability to initiate, view, and switch between multiple unique `event_id` records for different clients.
- **Role in Platform**: "The Professional Orchestrator."

## 2. Customer Lifecycle & Parity
The Event Planner has full access to the following Customer features:
- **Subscription Management**: Access to Silver, Gold, and Platinum billing tiers.
- **Token Management**: Purchasing and allocating tokens for high-volume ritual discovery.
- **Dashboard Synchronization**: Portfolio-level analytics for all managed events.

## 3. Metadata Requirements (Professional Profiling)
In addition to standard profile data, the Scribe uses these for professional branding:
- **Firm Name**: The planning agency's official trade name.
- **Lead Planner Name**: Individual contact managing the specific ritual.
- **Team Size**: Indicates the manpower available for on-ground coordination.
- **B2B Experience**: History of rituals managed within the platform.
