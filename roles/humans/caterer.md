# Blueprint: Caterer (Satvik & Vedic Meals)

The Caterer is the provider of nourishment and is vetted for high standards of Sattvik and Vedic menu authenticity.

## 1. Interaction Model
- **Discovery**: Qualified by the Finder Agent for menu authenticity and dietary compliance.
- **Supply Handshake**: Coordinates with the Supplies Agent for bulk procurement of pure ingredients (Ghee, grains, spices).
- **Role in Platform**: "The Food Specialist."

## 2. Metadata Requirements (For Scribe Agent)
The Scribe Agent needs these to populate the "Menu & Feast" section of the Event Website:
- **Lead Chef/Caterer Name**: Brand or individual name.
- **Cuisine Specialties**: E.g., South Indian Vedic, Sattvik Buffet, North Indian Traditional, Modern Fusion.
- **Sattvik Availability**: Explicitly state if the full menu can be prepared as **Sattvik** (no onion/garlic, Vedic compliant) or if they also offer high-quality **Traditional/Regular** options.
- **Menu Nature**: Ability to pivot based on the event's ritual requirements vs. celebratory needs.
- **Menu PDF/Visuals**: Full menu and photos of past event spreads.
- **Minimum/Maximum Plate Count**: For logistical planning.
- **License/FSSAI Info**: For safety and vetting logs.
